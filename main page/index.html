About Us

ABCO - International Academy of Accounting and Finance was established in 2009 as Right Accounting
LLC and has been providing quality accounting and consulting services since its establishment in 2016.

ABCO - International Academy of Accounting and Finance has provided services to more than 20,000 companies and organizations, and has repeatedly been recognized for its high level of service at various exhibitions. Indeed, 11 years of experience speak for themselves.

In 2017, NTM “Accounting and business courses” was established under “Right accounting” LLC. The main mission of the NTM "Accounting and business courses" is to train qualified accountants and financiers for the society. ek.
